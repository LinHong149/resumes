We're seeking Software Engineering Interns to join our Internal Engineering team, a group that builds the tools, applications, and infrastructure that power MongoDB's internal operations. The team partners closely with cross-functional stakeholders across the company - especially our go-to-market (GTM) and business systems teams - to improve productivity and leverage AI to make data more actionable.



Interns will work on projects that span both application development and infrastructure, contributing to systems that enhance internal decision-making and streamline workflows across departments. You'll gain hands-on experience working with AI-driven applications, APIs, and internal tooling used by MongoDB employees every day.



What we are looking for in you



In the process of receiving a Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, or a related field.

Have a foundation in computer science theory, with proficiencies in data structures, algorithms, and software design.

Strong programming foundation, particularly in Python (used across most projects); familiarity with Go and React a plus.

Interest in AI, LLMs, and automation, especially how they can improve internal tools and systems

A desire to grow as a full-stack engineer and contribute to meaningful, cross-functional projects that directly impact internal teams.

Demonstrated ownership, communication skills, and openness to taking on new challenges in a collaborative environment.

Have a minimum of one quarter/semester remaining in university studies after the internship concludes. Graduation date in Fall 2026 or Spring 2027.

Must be authorized to work in the United States. Company sponsorship may be available for eligible candidates applying for certain roles.
